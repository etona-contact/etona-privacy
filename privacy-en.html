Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Etona ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use our mobile application Etona (the "App"). By using the App you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Data we collect

  • Account info: email, display name, profile photo (optional).
  • Contact info: phone number, address (optional).
  • Appointments: booking date/time, barber id, client id, status.
  • Device data: device type, OS version, analytics for app performance.

Why we collect data

  • To provide and manage appointments and user profiles.
  • To send booking confirmations and status updates.
  • To improve the app and fix bugs.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

How we share data

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) who process data on our behalf.
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety.
Note: Barber public profile information (name, services, opening hours) is visible to app users so clients can browse and book.

Data retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide the service and to meet legal obligations. Appointment records may be retained for operational and support reasons. You can request deletion by contacting us.

Your rights

  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to processing where applicable.
